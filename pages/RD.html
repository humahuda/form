R & D

Our Institute, Bheemanna Khandre Institute of Technology Bhalki, has well equipped Research Centres in Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry. Currently 52 research scholars are pursuing their research at our Institute. The thrust areas of research include Medical Imaging, Optics, Photonics, VLSI, Material Science, Microwave Communication, Mechanical, Radiation Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry. We are currently executing projects worth 3.10 Crore sanctioned by VGST and Dept. IT, BT, Govt. of Karnataka. Also, the Institute has Centre of Excellence in Polymer Science, Centre for Innovation in Mathematics and different K-FIST programmes. Our faculty members have many publications in National & International Journals and Conferences indexed in Scopus, Web of Science and Google Scholar.
